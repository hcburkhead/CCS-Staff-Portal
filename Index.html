Fundraising Form

Thank you for your interest in developing a fundraiser to support the students and ministries of Cincinnati Christian Schools.

In order to have the fundraiser properly reviewed and approved, please complete the requested information below. Do not begin any extensive planning for your fundraiser before it is approved.

If and when your fundraiser has been approved, you will be notified via email. This request must be approved before any announcements/advertising may occur. Please note that we fully trust each and every organization conducting a fundraiser to properly thank and recognize those who support them in the most appropriate manner possible.

This form should be submitted a minimum of 3 weeks prior to start of your campaign/event. Less time sets up a situation for a less successful event. Appropriate timing for promotions is key!
